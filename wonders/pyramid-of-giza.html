The Great Pyramid Of Giza

The Great Pyramid of Giza (also known, and referred to as the "Pyramid of Khufu" or the "Pyramid of Cheops") is the oldest and largest Pyramid in the group of Pyramids on the outskirts of Giza, Greater Cairo, Egypt. It's the oldest of the "Seven Ancient Wonders of the World", and is the only one to be in, mostly, good shape.
