About

This site gives you information on the "7 Wonders Of The Ancient World" as defined by Herodotus and Callimachus of Cyrene, at the Museum Of Alexandria, over 2000 Years ago.

This site is here as a project, showcasing basic web development skills, using HTML 5, Cascading Style Sheets, and JavaScript.
